Game Rules

How to Play

Each player rolls the dice for every turn they take. If you get an answer wrong, move 2 spaces back. If you get it right, you may stay on the square you are on.

Cards and Spaces

  • ? space: Players must pick up a mystery card from the deck and read it out loud.
  • ! space: Players may move 3 spaces forwards (normal mode) or choose a person to pick a mystery card (hard mode).

Game Modes

Normal Mode

The "!" space allows the player to move 3 spaces forwards.

Hard Mode

The "!" space allows the player to choose a person to pick a mystery card.

Reward

At the end of the game, the first person to reach the finish line gets to pick up a card for the last person that made it and read it out loud so they can hear. If they get it wrong, then that person has sinned.
